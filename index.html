House hacking is the strategy of living in a property while renting out part of it. Your tenant pays your mortgage while you build equity.

At 5 Barnstable, you'll live upstairs in complete privacy while renting the downstairs unit. The rental income of $2,900/month covers most of your mortgage, taxes, and insurance.
