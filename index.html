What is House Hacking?

House hacking is a brilliant real estate strategy where you live in one unit of a multi-unit property (or rent out part of your home) while renting out the other unit(s) to tenants. The rental income you receive helps offset or even completely cover your mortgage payment, property taxes, and insurance.

At 5 Barnstable, you can live in the comfortable upstairs unit with your own kitchen, living area, and private balcony, while renting out the first-floor unit to a tenant. This means you're building equity while someone else helps pay your mortgage!

Benefits of House Hacking

  • Dramatically Reduce Housing Costs: Live for a fraction of what you'd normally pay in rent or mortgage payments.
  • Build Wealth Faster: Someone else helps pay down your mortgage principal while you build equity.
  • Owner-Occupant Financing: Qualify for better loan terms with lower down payments (as low as 3.5% with FHA).
  • Learn Real Estate Investing: Get hands-on landlord experience while living on-site.
  • Tax Benefits: Deduct rental property expenses and depreciation (consult your tax advisor).
  • Flexibility: Have a family member or friend stay in one unit, or maximize rental income with a tenant.
